Yellow Rice & Chicken

Ingredients

  • Boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup long grain white rice
  • 2 large yellow onions
  • Red cabbage
  • Grape tomatoes
  • Parsley
  • Red Onion
  • Tahini
  • Red Wine Vinegar
  • Lemon Juice
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Tumeric
  • Cumin
  • Cayenne Pepper

Steps to follow

  1. Add olive oil, lemon juice, salt, pepper, tumeric, cumin, cayenne pepper to a bowl
  2. Add the chicken to that marinade and let marinate
  3. Chop the onions into small cubes
  4. Sear the chicken with some olive oil until about 70% cooked
  5. Remove the chicken and add the chopped onions into the pot
  6. Add salt, minced garlic and tumeric and cook until onions start to yellow
  7. Add 1 cup of long grain rice along with 1.75 cups of water and bring to a boil
  8. Add the chicken back to the pot and cook on low for 15-18 minutes
  9. Chop the red cabbage, red onions, tomatoes, parsley and add to a bowl
  10. Add red wine vinegar, olive oil, salt, and pepper and mix
  11. In a seperate bowl add tahini, water, lemon juice and salt to make a dressing
  12. Plate the rice, followed by the chicken, cabbage slaw and then drizzle the tahini dressing on top
  13. Last but not least, Enjoy!
